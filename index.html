Fruit Cheesecake

This red fruit cheesecake recipe is simply delicious! The combination of the sweetness of the strawberry with the slightly acidic taste of the blueberry creates a fresh and balanced dessert. Moreover, the creamy texture of the cheesecake perfectly contrasts with the crunch of the cookie base. If you're looking for an easy-to-make dessert that will impress your guests, this is the ideal recipe

How to Prepare

Ingredients:

  • 200g of Maisena biscuits
  • 100g of butter
  • 300g of cream cheese
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of unflavored gelatin powder
  • 1 cup of chopped strawberries
  • 1 cup of blueberries

Instructions

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix with the melted butter. Line a mold and refrigerate.
  2. Beat the cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, and lemon juice. Add the dissolved gelatin
  3. Pour over the biscuit base and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  4. Top with the fruit and serve chilled.
